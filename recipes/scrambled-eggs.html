Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a delicious and easy breakfast. Follow the steps below to make them perfectly every time.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs.
  2. Add salt to the beat eggs and mix.
  3. Preheat nonstick pan.
  4. Add butter and melt.
  5. Add eggs and cook, stirring until done.
